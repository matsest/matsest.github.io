About Me

Hi! Welcome to my personal digital corner of the Internet!

I am an IT Infrastructure Engineer and Consultant working in Oslo, Norway.

Currently, I am on 4-month educational leave while I am pursuing a master’s thesis within physics/nanoelectronics. I previously studied nanotechnology in NTNU, Trondheim with one year abroad at University of California Santa Barbara. Along my studies I worked part-time and had internships within IT development and operations, which led me into the IT business.

I am interested in Linux, cloud computing, virtualization, automation/devops, information security, privacy tools, pentesting, general programming and more.

I support free and open-source software, digital privacy and an open and democratic Internet. I am slowly starting to contributing more to open-source, so check out my profile on GitHub to see my activity (sadly not much from my day job – yet).

While I am not busy working I enjoy going to concerts and listen to music, jumping out of perfectly good planes, and enjoying life – all while looking out for new things to learn.

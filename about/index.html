About Me

Hi! Welcome to my personal digital corner of the Internet!

I am a Cloud Infrastructure Engineer and Consultant working in Oslo, Norway. Since 2017 I have been working in the Digital Platform Services division at Sopra Steria. At the moment I am mainly working with developing public cloud infrastructure solutions on AWS and Azure through infrastructure-as-code tools like terraform.

From 2012 to 2017 I studied nanotechnology (M.Sc.) at NTNU, Trondheim with one year abroad at the University of California Santa Barbara. Along my studies I worked part-time with IT infrastructure development and operations for three years, which led me into the IT business with a summer internship at Sopra Steria in 2016.

I am interested in cloud, infrastructure-as-code, automation, DevOps, Linux, information security, general programming and much more. See more on my GitHub profile.

I support free and open-source software, digital privacy and an open and democratic Internet.

While I am not busy working I enjoy going to concerts and listen to music, jumping out of perfectly good planes, and enjoying life – all while looking out for new things to learn.
